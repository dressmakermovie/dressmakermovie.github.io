The Dressmaker (2015)

A bittersweet comedy about love, revenge, and haute couture. Based on the novel by Rosalie Ham.



Watch the trailer on YouTube

The Dressmaker boasts a strong central performance by Kate Winslet and a captivating array of narrative weirdness — all of which may or may not be a comfortable fit with viewers.


Accused of murder when she was a child, a dressmaker (Kate Winslet) returns to her small Australian town to seek revenge on the locals who did her wrong.

Starring Kate Winslet, Judy Davis, and Liam Hemsworth
